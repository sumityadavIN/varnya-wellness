What is Hypertension (Raktachapa)?

In Ayurveda, Hypertension is often referred to as Raktachapa. It is not viewed merely as a cardiovascular issue but as an imbalance involving the Vyaana Vata (responsible for circulation) and Prana Vata (mental calm).

When these energies are disturbed, often due to stress, poor diet, or toxins (Ama), it creates pressure in the blood vessels. Ayurveda seeks to address the "silent killer" by cooling the blood and grounding the nervous system.
