What is Infertility in Ayurveda?

Ayurveda views fertility as a reflection of overall health. Successful conception requires four essential factors: Ritu (Timing/Seasons), Kshetra (The Field/Uterus), Ambu (Nourishment/Hormones), and Beeja (The Seed/Ovum & Sperm).

Infertility, or Vandhyatva, often arises when imbalances in the Doshas (especially Vata) affect any of these four factors, or when Ama (toxins) block the reproductive channels (Shukra Vaha Srotas).
