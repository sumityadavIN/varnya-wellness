What are Skin Disorders in Ayurveda?

Ayurveda classifies most skin diseases under the broad heading of Kushta. Unlike modern dermatology which often focuses on topical applications, Ayurveda views skin issues as a manifestation of internal imbalances, specifically involving the Rakta (blood) and Mamsa (tissues).

When Pitta and Kapha doshas become aggravated, they mix with toxins (Ama) and settle in the skin, leading to inflammation, itching, discoloration, and chronic conditions like Eczema, Psoriasis, and Acne.
