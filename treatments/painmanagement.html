What is Chronic Pain in Ayurveda?

In Ayurveda, pain is primarily associated with an aggravation of Vata Dosha. When the flow of energy is obstructed by Ama (toxins) or physical tension, it results in discomfort, stiffness, and chronic inflammation.

Whether it is lower back pain, cervical spondylosis, or arthritis, Ayurveda views pain not just as a local symptom, but as a systemic imbalance. Our goal is to lubricate the joints, calm the nervous system, and restore mobility.
