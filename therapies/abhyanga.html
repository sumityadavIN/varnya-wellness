What is Abhyangam?

Abhyanga is the flagship Ayurvedic massage therapy involving the rhythmic application of warm, medicated oils over the entire body. It is designed to penetrate deep into the tissues to release toxins and calm the nervous system.

By synchronizing the massage strokes with the direction of arterial blood flow, Abhyanga boosts circulation, enhances lymphatic drainage, and provides a "protective shield" against stress and aging.
