What is Swedana?

Swedana is a specialized Ayurvedic steam therapy used to induce sweating, which helps in thinning the body's toxins and moving them out through the pores of the skin.

By using herbal-infused steam, Swedana liquefies deep-seated impurities (Ama) and improves peripheral circulation. It is most commonly used as a follow-up to Abhyanga massage to ensure that the medicated oils penetrate even deeper into the tissues while relaxing the entire musculoskeletal system.
