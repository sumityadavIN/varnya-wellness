What is Raktamokshana?

Raktamokshana is one of the five primary Panchakarma therapies, specifically aimed at neutralizing and expelling vitiated Pitta and toxins (Ama) from the bloodstream.

In Ayurveda, "Rakta" (blood) is the carrier of life energy. When blood becomes impure due to metabolic waste or systemic imbalances, it manifests as skin diseases, chronic inflammation, and circulatory issues. Raktamokshana effectively removes these localized toxins to restore systemic health.
