Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit

How long does treatment take to show results?

Most patients notice improvements within 2-3 weeks. Chronic conditions may require 6-12 weeks for complete healing. We provide realistic timelines during your consultation based on your specific condition.

Is Panchakarma safe? Are there side effects?

Panchakarma is completely safe when administered by qualified doctors. Our BAMS-certified practitioners follow traditional protocols. You may experience mild detox symptoms (fatigue, headache) for 1-2 days as toxins are eliminated - this is normal and temporary.

What's the consultation fee and treatment cost?

Initial consultation: ₹1000 (includes pulse diagnosis and personalized treatment plan). Treatment costs vary based on your condition. Panchakarma programs typically range from ₹15,000-₹45,000. We provide detailed cost breakdown before you start.

Can I continue my current medications?

Yes! Ayurveda works alongside modern medicine. We'll review your current medications and create a safe, integrated treatment plan. Many patients gradually reduce dependency on medications as their condition improves naturally.

Do I need to stay at the clinic during treatment?

No residential stay required. Most treatments are outpatient - you visit for 1-2 hours daily. For intensive Panchakarma (7-21 days), we can arrange nearby accommodation if you're traveling from outside Gurugram.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring: (1) Any recent medical reports/test results (2) List of current medications (3) Comfortable clothing for examination (4) Questions about your health concerns. First visit takes 45-60 minutes including pulse diagnosis and treatment planning.
