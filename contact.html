Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability. For urgent consultations, please call us directly.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any medical records, recent test results, and a list of current medications. Wear comfortable clothing for pulse diagnosis.

How long does a consultation take?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes. This includes pulse diagnosis, health history review, and personalized treatment plan.

Do you accept insurance?

We provide detailed invoices that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement. Please check with your provider about Ayurvedic treatment coverage.
