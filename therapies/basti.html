What is Basti?

Basti is a specialized Ayurvedic medicated enema therapy. In Ayurveda, the colon is the primary seat of Vata Dosha. Basti is considered the most powerful treatment for balancing Vata and eliminating deep-seated toxins from the entire body.

By introducing herbal decoctions or medicated oils directly into the colon, Basti nourishes the system, strengthens the gut-brain axis, and addresses the root cause of 80% of systemic diseases including joint issues and neurological disorders.
