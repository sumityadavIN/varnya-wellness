What is Ayurvedic Weight Loss?

In Ayurveda, weight gain is not just about calories; it is an accumulation of Meda Dhatu (excess fat tissue) caused by a weakened Agni (digestive fire). When Agni is sluggish, the body produces Ama (toxins) that block the metabolic channels.

Ayurvedic weight loss is the process of detoxifying these channels, rekindling the metabolic fire, and shifting the body from "storage mode" to "energy mode." It is a holistic restoration of your biology rather than a temporary fix.
