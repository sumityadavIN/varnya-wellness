What is Shirodhara?

Shirodhara (from 'Shiro' meaning head and 'Dhara' meaning flow) is a unique Ayurvedic procedure where a continuous stream of warm, medicated oil is poured onto the forehead, specifically over the 'Third Eye' or Ajna Chakra.

This rhythmic flow induces a profound state of relaxation, balancing the endocrine system and regulating the central nervous system. It is widely considered the most effective Ayurvedic treatment for mental exhaustion and stress-related disorders.
