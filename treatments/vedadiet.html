What is Metabolic Imbalance?

In Ayurveda, health is governed by Agni (digestive fire). When Agni is weak or irregular, it leads to metabolic imbalance, resulting in the accumulation of Ama—undigested toxic waste that clogs the body's channels.

This imbalance manifests as persistent fatigue, weight gain, digestive distress, and a "foggy" mind. Veda Diet addresses this at the root by rekindling your internal fire.
